Installing a Story

  1. Download the Story
  2. Open StoryCraft
  3. Press Play
  4. Press New Story
  5. Press Open templates folder
  6. Copy the Path
  7. Open your downloaded story

    You can usally do that from your browsers Download tab. Or open Explorer and load the Downloads folder

  8. Press extract all
  9. Paste the path you copied before
  10. Done!
